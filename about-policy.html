Policy

With the responsibility of realizing a bright future, NRI always makes efforts to excite the toughest tasks.

Since IT (Information Technology) has become more important for the implementation of the business reform and operating strategy, NRI uses Navigation and Solutions as the core means to anticipate the issues that customers may encounter in the coming years and provides them with concrete and feasible solutions. This is what the NRI Total Solution is.


The core of the NRI Total Solution is the optimal "NRI Synergistic Power". It indicates a "broad category" and a professional meaning of "solving problems from synergistic and diverse points of view". With the continuously accumulated extensive experience and knowledge in the financial, logistic, public and other industrial areas, the NRI Group is capable of providing complete and effective services in different operating fields including operating strategies, business strategies, IT strategies, organizational and personnel reforms, improvement of corporate values and the development of technologies.


When solving problems for customers, we gather professional personnel from different departments and fields to form a team. The problems are then figured out, assessed, and analyzed from different points of view based on the extensive experience and knowledge of the various team members to provide the customer with the most unique solutions.


NRI uses careful insight and practice to build a brand new society. We are committed to becoming "an enterprise that creates and invents future societies", and will make unremitting efforts and face these challenges without fear.

Being the most trusted partner od clients, NRI continuously makes innovation for a brighter future.

Our basic operating policy is to "Create Added Value and Increase Corporate Values". Providing customers with highly value-added and advanced products is our mission, and, by doing so, we make a contribution to the business of our customers. We simultaneously assist our customers in the improvement of their corporate value as well as our own.


"Knowledge" is the main focus of today, and we are in an information society. Companies and individuals are striving for useful knowledge and information to solve their problems, meet their requirements, and create an ideal future.


We never forget to exert all its strength to meet the requirements of demanding customers and live up to an ideal future society.


For this, flexible and efficient integration of different optimal professional capacities is needed to provide highly value-added services for any issue at any time. We never let up in our self-improvment. In addition to the internal system rectification, such as enhancing quality control and project management capabilities, and the provision of innovative solutions, we continuously endeavor to train talents as the "valuables" of NRI who can reinforce our professional capabilities.


We dedicate ourselves to creating added value for our customers and improving their corporate value on an ongoing basis. We are committed to becoming their "most reliable partner" and making a contribution to the reengineering of the Japanese economy and enterprises.
