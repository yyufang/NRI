Department Overview

Consumer goods, Service industry consultation
  • Development of brand and marketing strategies
  • Positioning analysis, cusumer requirements analysis
  • Development of channel and customer strategies
  • Business process restructure
  • Overseas market entry strategy

Since the saturation of the domestic market , the brand owners and service providers are facing an increasingly fierce competition in the market. They must establish a highly strategic management system which aims to fulfill different target customer's requirements so as to maintain the growth in such a severe market environment. Since the Japanese market has experienced such a development, NRI Taiwan is capable of transferring various solutions from the Japanese market to Taiwan and helping the companies in Taiwan enhance their competitiveness in the domestic and global markets.

Social infrastructure consultation
  • Establishment of the land and urban development projects
  • Development of the policy in the public sector
  • Establishment of the master plan and strategy in the infracture area
  • Evaluation of business value for the participation of the private companies in the public construction projects

Since its establishment in 1994, NRI Taiwan has been authorized by the government to provide planning services for many land, urban, harbor, airport, railway and other transport facilities development projects. The investment of the private capital in public construction has become more prosperous in recent years, the private companies that participated in the projects by providing them with consultation service. In the social infrastructure area, we are able to provide one-stop highly professional service in the development of conceptions and master plans, assessment of business, financial analysis and other relevant fields.

Manufacturing consultation
  • Supporting service for the manufacturers in their investment in new business
  • Assisting Japan-based manufacturers in searching for partners in Taiwan
  • Development strategies of sales channels in the Greater China Region
  • Feasibility assessment of the investment in the Greater China Region

Taiwan may well be called the head of the global electornics manufacturing industry. When studying the new investment areas for the manufacturers in Taiwan, NRI not only analyzes the industrial structure, but also dissects successsful and failed cases in the global compitition, and provides consultation services regarding the investment targets and business model. Since Taiwan is the core in the core of the Greater China Region’s hub, more and more Japanese companies have established connections to China and Southeast Asia through Taiwan in recent years. NRI is capable of collecting extensive information via the overseas personal connections of our Taiwanese consultants to provide efficient consultation services for the Japan-based manufacturers and help them in the development of Taiwan’s market, assessment of partners, and development of their business in China and Southeast Asia.

JAPAN DESK, Support for Japanese companies to invest in Taiwan
  • Presentation of the economic status and investment environment in Taiwan as well as the development tendency of Japan-based companies
  • Provision of information such as laws and regulations, procedures and statistical data
  • Acting as a bridge between Taiwan governments and investors
  • Market survey, matchmaking, development of market entering strategies

JAPAN DESK is a project office helping Japanese companies to understand the investment environment in Taiwan. We have been authorized by the Ministry of Economic Affairs, R.O.C., to execute the project since 1995, and helped more than 200 Japanese companies to invest in Taiwan every year. We also make market survey of specific industries needed for the Japanese companies to enter Taiwan market, and provide consultation service to facilitate the matchmaking with local companies.
Japanese companies set a new record of 619 direct investments (US$ 410 million) in Taiwan in 2012.
